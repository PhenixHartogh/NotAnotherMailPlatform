Email Client Setup

Email Client Setup Instructions

Note: We do not currently support autodiscover or autoconfig. You will need to manually enter server settings in your email client.

Only secure SSL/TLS connections are supported. Unencrypted protocols are not allowed.

Incoming Mail (IMAP)

  • Server: mail.namp.xyz
  • Port: 993
  • Security: SSL/TLS
  • Authentication: Normal Password

Incoming Mail (POP3)

  • Server: mail.namp.xyz
  • Port: 995
  • Security: SSL/TLS
  • Authentication: Normal Password

Outgoing Mail (SMTP)

  • Server: mail.namp.xyz
  • Port: 465
  • Security: SSL/TLS
  • Authentication: Required
  • Username: your full email address

If your client asks for username and password for sending/receiving, use your full email address and password you created during signup.
