Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Not Another Mail Platform (NAMP) handles your personal data.

Server Location

All data is hosted on servers located in Sydney, Australia.

No IP Logging

We do not log IP addresses of users who access our services, including webmail and signup forms.

Data Encryption

We cannot view your email contents. All user email data is stored on our server in encrypted form and cannot be accessed by NAMP staff.

Session Cookies

We use browser cookies to manage user sessions and maintain login states. These cookies do not track you across the internet and are only used for service functionality.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics to help us understand traffic patterns and improve our website. This service may collect anonymised usage data, subject to Google’s privacy policy.

Data Collection

We do not collect or store any personal information beyond your email address and encrypted password. We do not store names, phone numbers, or IP addresses.

Third-Party Access

No third parties have access to your data. Your information is never shared, sold, or transferred outside NAMP systems.

Hosting Provider

NAMP is hosted on infrastructure provided by VentraIP Australia.

Security & Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach or unauthorised access to NAMP’s systems, we will notify affected users within 1–3 weeks via their NAMP email address.

Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time without notice. Users are encouraged to review this page periodically.
